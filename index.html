About Nitin Foam & Handloom

Nitin Associates Pvt. Ltd., the premier Mattress Store in Modinagar, is where value meets style. Since 1996 we’ve provided our customers with a wide selection of affordable home good products.


With our extensive inventory, we have something for every taste and every budget. Our buyers scour the world looking for the latest in home styling trends. The result are options you just can’t find anywhere else. Come down to our conveniently located Mattress Store to see our full selection of products.

Our Collection

Our wide range of mattresses caters to different preferences and needs, ensuring that you find the perfect match for a restful slumber. From memory foam to orthopedic mattresses, we offer options that provide optimal comfort and support for your body.


In addition to our extensive mattress collection, Step into our store, and you'll be greeted with a delightful assortment of bedsheets, cushions, pillows, blankets, and quilts
